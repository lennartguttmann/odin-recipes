Spaghetti Carbonara

Indredients

  • Spaghetti 200g
  • Oilive oil
  • Eggs 2
  • Guancale (an italian ham)
  • Pecorino cheese

How to make the carbonara ?

Cook the spaghetti until they are a little bit stiffer than "aldente"

While cooking the spaghetti fry the pecorino in a pan with olive oil. Stir the eggs and add grated pecorino to it

If the spaghetti are ready, put them directly into the pan and add the mixture of eggs and pecorino to it

Turn down the heat of the pan und cook until the pasta is aldente
