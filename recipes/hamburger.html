Delicious Hamburger ?

Ingredients

  • Gras fed beef: 1 1/2 pounds
  • Onions: 1/3
  • Brioche bread: 1 piece
  • Ketchup: as much as you like
  • Pickle: 1 piece
  • Tomato: 1/2 piece medium size

How to make the best burger?

  1. Grill the Beff medium.
  2. Cut an fry the onion until it's golden brown.
  3. Cut the pickle and the tomate
  4. Cut the brioche in two halfs und put it for a minute on top of your toaster
  5. After toasting your Bread put ketchup on it and add the onions, pickles and tomato on one half of the bread.
  6. At last add the beef and close it with the second half
